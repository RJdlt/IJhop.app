Help

Everything you need to know before and after your order. If anything is unclear, just write to us. A real person will answer.

Delivery

Complimentary over 1,500 kr. We pack carefully and let you know when your piece is on its way.

Returns

30 days. If a piece is not right for your home, send it back and we will make it easy.

Care

Natural materials last when looked after. We include simple care notes with every order.

Where do you ship?

Sweden and the rest of the Nordics to start, with more of Europe to follow. The final shipping options and prices will be confirmed with the real launch.

How long does delivery take?

Smaller pieces usually arrive within a few working days. Larger furniture takes a little longer, and we will always give you a clear estimate at checkout.

What is your return policy?

You have 30 days to return a piece in its original condition. Reach out and we will guide you through it, calmly and without fuss.

How do I care for solid wood and linen?

A soft cloth and the occasional refresh of oil keeps oak beautiful for decades. Linen softens and improves with washing. Full care notes come with your order.

Are your materials sustainable?

We choose natural, traceable materials and design pieces to be kept and repaired rather than replaced. We are happy to tell you exactly where things come from.

How can I reach you?

Write to us on the contact page. We read every message ourselves.

Shipping zones, prices and policy details will be finalised with the real launch.
