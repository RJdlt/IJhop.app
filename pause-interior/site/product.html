The story of this piece

Made slowly, kept for years

Oak is patient. It darkens a little with the light and the years, and it carries a mark or two as proof of a life lived around it. We chose it because it lasts, and because it feels honest under your hand.

Each table is finished with a natural oil you can refresh yourself, so it stays beautiful for decades rather than seasons.
